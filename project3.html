3D Rendering


Since 2016 I have been remodeling buildings & houses, specializing in kitchens and bathrooms. Seeing a 3D rendering of your project BEFORE you commit to the "after" results can be a bright lantern in the dark when it comes to remodeling projects. Rendering services are available as well as Interior Design services on a room-by-room basis.

The design phases for this process are:

Programming: A thorough discussion on what you are trying to achieve with your project, your styles & preferences, and the scope of work.
Schematic Design: The floor plan and general direction of the project are formed and agreed upon.
Design Development: In this phase products are chosen and secured. It is up to the client how involved they are with this phase. Some clients prefer to be completely hands off and some clients choose to consult with me, but research & select each finish themselves.
Construction Documents: For some projects this might just be 3D renderings and a floor plan. For permitted projects, the required architectural construction documents are drawn.
Construction Administration: The phase when your project comes together and we make sure it's going correctly. This includes site visits and communication with the builder(s) and installer(s). This phase ends with happy clients and photos of a job well done.
